Overview of Groww

Groww Demat Account Opening and AMC Charges in 2023

Groww provides a Free Demat Account opening process that is completely online and takes less than 15 ⏱️ minutes to complete. There are no account opening fees, and it has no Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC) for life. 💰.

Groww Brokerage Charges Explained

Groww – Equity Delivery & Intraday Charges

For equity delivery & intraday Groww’s brokerage is charged at Rs 20 per executed order or 0.05% of the order value (whichever is lower).

Groww – Futures & Options Charges

For Futures & Options the brokerage Fee charged Flat at Rs.20/ Executed order.

How to Open Groww Account Online ?

You can follow these steps to open a Groww Demat & Trading Account online,

  1. 📲 Download the Groww App and log in.
  2. Navigate to the ‘Stocks’ tab and click on ‘complete setup’.
  3. 💰 Click on ‘Open Stocks Account‘ Account Opening Charges in Groww is Zero.
  4. 📝 Complete the KYC process by providing details about your PAN, occupation, income, and parents’ names.
  5. 📊 Select your Trading Experience from list and click ‘Next’.
  6. 🖋️ Upload your signature on a plain white sheet of paper. Ensure that it is clearly visible and click on ‘Looks Good‘ to proceed.
  7. 🔐 Use the Aadhaar based e-sign process by submitting your Aadhaar number and receiving an OTP on your registered mobile number. Click on ‘E-SIGN’ to proceed.
  8. 📲 If mobile number is linked to your Aadhaar, enter the OTP/security code sent to your mobile and click ‘Submit’.
  9. 📜 Carefully read the Demat account opening form and click ‘Sign Now’.
  10. 🔑 Enter your Aadhaar number or Aadhaar virtual ID, and complete the e-sign process at NSDL Signing Platform by entering the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
  11. ✅ You will see a success screen that says ‘Signed successfully’, which means account opening process is completed successfully

⏳Please wait for the verification process to be completed within 24 hours. Once your KYC is verified by the Groww backend team, you will receive an email and text message confirming that your Demat account with Groww has been successfully opened.
