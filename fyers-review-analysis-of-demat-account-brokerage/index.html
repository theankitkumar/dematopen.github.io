Overview of Fyers

Fyers Demat Account Opening and AMC Charges in 2024

Fyers offers 🆓 free demat account opening that is entirely 🌐 online, with no account opening fees, and that takes less than 15 ⏱️ minutes to complete. It has No Annual maintenance charges (AMC) for life. 💰

Fyers Brokerage Charges Explained

Fyers – Equity Delivery & Intraday Charges

Equities delivery brokerage is Zero in Fyers . intraday brokerage is Rs 20 per executed order or 0.03%. (whichever is lower).

Fyers – Futures & Options Charges

For Futures, the brokerage fee is Rs 20 (0.03%) for each executed trade (whichever is less). however options Are charged Flat at Rs.20/ order

Fyers – Currency Charges

Fyers Currency’s Charges is Rs 20 per executed order, which is 0.03%. (whichever is lower) for Futures and ₹ 20 Per Executed Order for options

Fyers – Commodity Charges

Fyers Commodity Charges is Rs 20 per executed order, which is 0.03%. (whichever is lower) for Futures and ₹ 20 Per Executed Order for options

Fyers Trading Platforms

Fyers Web 💻

Fyers Web is a web-based platform that provides advanced trading tools and features. It includes a Tradingview Library 📊, direct trading via charts 📈, real-time ticker for bids/offers 💰, and readymade watchlists 📜. It also offers advanced charting with 24+ years of EOD data 📉 and real-time configurable option chains ⛓️.

Fyers App 📱

Fyers App is a mobile trading platform that offers direct trade and investing with features like two-factor authentication 🔒 for secure transactions, customizable watchlists 📋, and advanced charts with technical indicators 📈 and multiple drawing tools 📏.

Fyers One 💻

Fyers One is a desktop-based trading platform that provides features like a Heat Map 🌡️, Market Dynamics 📊, and Index Meter 📈 to help users understand market-wide movements and trends. Fyers Direct 💰

Fyers Direct is a mutual fund investment platform that allows users to invest in mutual funds directly from their Demat Account 💳 at no extra cost. It also offers the opportunity to invest in top companies and international stocks 🌍, view all mutual fund holdings in one place 📈, and redeem mutual fund corpus with a single click 💰.

Fyers Thematic 🌱

Fyers Thematic allows users to invest in ready-made portfolios that follow specific investment styles or themes, such as technology 💻, healthcare 🏥, or renewable energy 🌞. It offers zero joining or signup fees 🆓, the ability to create and track portfolios based on specific themes or sectors 📈, and customized alerts for specific themes or sectors 🔔.

Steps to Open Fyers Account Online

If you want to open a Fyers Demat & Trading Account online, you can follow these steps: 📈

  1. Go to the official Fyers website 🔍
  2. Click on the Signup Now 📝
  3. Enter your email and mobile number 📱
  4. Click on the “Send an OTP” option and verify your details by entering the OTP 🔑
  5. Upload your PAN card photo or submit your KYC documents from DigiLocker to provide your identity details 🆔
  6. Enter your Aadhaar card number and OTP to fetch KYC documents from DigiLocker 🔑
  7. Provide basic details such as father name, mother name, gender, and marital status 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
  8. Submit your address proof 🏠
  9. Add your bank account details and select your choice of segment/s to activate 💳
  10. If you want to trade in the derivatives segment, you’ll need to upload a financial income proof 💰
  11. Provide additional information and add a nominee to your account (optional) 👤
  12. Record a video of yourself with a clearly written OTP on plain white paper to go through the in-person/live video verification process 📹
  13. Upload your photograph and signature 📷
  14. Enter your Aadhaar number and verify it with OTP to proceed to the e-sign now option 🔑✍️

Note: Once you have completed all the steps, your application will go under review, and the Fyers team will notify you via a welcome email that contains your account details 📧

📝 Documents Requirements For Online Fyers Account Opening

If you want to open a demat account with Fyers, you’ll need to provide the following documents:
