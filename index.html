AngelOne Demat Account Frequently Asked Questions

What are the charges for opening an account? Is it free?

Demat account opening is free of cost. However, there are additional charges:
Annual Maintenance Charge (AMC): Rs 0 during the first year and Rs 20 per month + taxes thereafter.
One-time charge of Rs. 50/- for POA + taxes.
Rs. 36/- for KRA along with applicable service taxes.

What documents are required for Demat Account opening?

To open a Demat account with Angel One, it's a paperless process. You'll need documents like PAN card, Adhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License for proof of identity, and Passport, Voter ID, Utility Bills, Bank Passbook, or other documents for proof of address.

Who can open a Demat Account?

Any Indian resident with the necessary identification, address proof, and income proof can open a Demat account to buy and sell securities in Indian stock exchanges.

What types of securities can I hold in a Demat account?

You can hold various financial securities, including equity shares, bonds, debentures, mutual funds, ETFs, and government securities, in a Demat account.

Can I apply for IPOs using a Demat Account?

Yes, you need a Demat account to receive IPO shares. The process is digital, and you can open one quickly to apply for IPOs.

What are the features & benefits of a Demat account?

A Demat account reduces paperwork, provides secure and convenient tracking of shares, enables quick share transfers, and eliminates the risk of stolen, damaged, or forged paper certificates.
