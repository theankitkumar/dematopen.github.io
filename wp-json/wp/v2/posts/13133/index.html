Do you have a demat account<\/strong> with Upstox, Zerodha, AngelOne, or any other stock broker? If Yes, this could be important for you.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you are an investor or Trader<\/strong> and you do not have a nominee to your demat account <\/strong>as of March 31, 2023<\/strong>, your trading and demat accounts will be frozen. <\/strong>All Demat and trading account holders are required by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) to choose a nominee by March 31, 2023. <\/strong>In the event that investors miss the deadline, their Demat account will be frozen. Previously, the last date<\/strong> for submitting nominations was March 31, 2022.<\/strong> The deadline was later extended by SEBI by one year. <\/strong>Investors who have already Submitted nomination <\/strong>details to their Stock Broker are not required to resubmit the nomination details.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

For new investors who want to Open a new trading and Demat account<\/a><\/strong>, a declaration form will let them choose whether to make a nomination or not. <\/strong>Also, no witnesses will be needed to file the nomination for trading and Demat accounts if the account holder signs the form in person.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span>Why Should you Add Nominee to Your Demat Account ?<\/span><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

    Having a nominee for your demat account is essential<\/strong> since it guarantees that your securities will be transferred to that person in the case of your death.<\/strong> If you name a receiver or nominee, they will receive your securities directly from you, without the proceedings.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    In the event that you do not add a nominee to your demat account, your loved ones may be forced to deal with a great deal of difficulty<\/strong>. In other situations, investments may remain frozen for years, and your family may be unable to access them at all.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Here are the steps to add a nominee to your Upstox<\/a>, Zerodha, Angelone or any Stock Broker Demat Account.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n

    3. On the nominee details page, choose to “add nominee”<\/strong> or “opt-out.<\/strong>“<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    4. Fill in the nominee’s details and upload their ID proof.<\/strong><\/li>\n\n\n\n

    6. E-sign<\/strong> the document with an Aadhaar OTP.<\/strong><\/li>\n\n\n\n
    7. The nominee will be added to the Demat account within 24-48 hours.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n
